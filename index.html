Welcome!

The objectives of Nepean Songsters are to enjoy choral singing, make new friends and participate in the choir's annual concert and community sing-outs.

Founded in 1993, Nepean Songsters are known around the Ottawa, Ontario, area for their entertaining choral performances at various retirement residences, nursing homes and special events. Nepean Songsters are presently led by Musical Director Saundra Vandenberg and Accompanist Carla Klassen.

The highlight of Nepean Songstersâ€™ season is their annual Fall Concert in the Centrepointe Studio at Ben Franklin Place, Nepean, Ontario. To prepare for this event and their various sing-outs, the members practice every Thursday morning at 9 to 11 AM, from September to May, at the Nepean Seniors Recreation Centre, Nepean, Ontario.
